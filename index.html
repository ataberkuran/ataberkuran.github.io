Ataberk Uran

Co Founder / Game Designer at Snapshell

Born on a rainy day in October 1995, Ataberk was always interested in computers. In early years of school time, he went to LAN cafes to play with his friends and during high school years he started thinking, “What if I play the game I made with my friends, that would be a cool thing to do!” This idea pushed him towards designing games. Ambitious high schooler merged with his creative side and quickly he started to understand the concepts of the early game design process. He attended to his first game jam during this period, which is Global Game Jam 2013 Istanbul @BUG, he created his first ever finished game. Later that he continued to participate in other Game Jams such as Indies vs PewDiePie Game Jam and many other Global Game Jams. He attended to DePaul University as a Game Design major. Currently he is looking for a job where he can call home.
