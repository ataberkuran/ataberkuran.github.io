Duel It Yourself

DUEL IT YOURSELF

Game made for the Global Game Jam 2020. It is a two-player westen duel in space. Rebuild your weapon quickly, aim and fire. Be careful about the gravity, and if you miss, you have to repair your gun all over again. First player to hit the other player 3 times wins.

I have worked with a group throughout the Global Game Jam 2020. During the Global Game Jam, I came up with the concept of the game according to the theme which was “Repair”. I pitched the idea and quickly me and my team got to work. Limited time in the Global Game Jam pushed us to 2 sleepless nights but at the end we finished the game which we are proud of to this day.

After Global Game Jam we returned and took a look at the game once again. It was a great concept and we wanted to build on it even further on our own time. That’s when we came up with the idea of bringing this game to the VR, and made the prototype for it in Unreal Engine 4.

For the VR project, I came up with the conceptualizing of the game in VR. What can we change, what can we add, and what can we further improve in the project. After the conceptualizing phase I mainly focused on modeling on top of the little design tweaks along the way.
